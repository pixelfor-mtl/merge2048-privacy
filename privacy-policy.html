To support the App with advertising, we use Google AdMob, which may collect certain device information, including your advertising identifier (Advertising ID), to serve and personalize ads and measure ad performance.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google's data collection and use practices are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not directly collect or store personal data on our own servers. Any data processed through third-party services (such as AdMob) is handled according to those providers' own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Vi indsamler eller opbevarer ikke direkte personoplysninger på vores egne servere. Data, der behandles via tredjepartstjenester (såsom AdMob), håndteres i henhold til disse udbyderes egne privatlivspraksisser.

Appen er ikke rettet mod børn under 13 år, og vi indsamler ikke bevidst personlige oplysninger fra børn. Hvis du mener, at et barn har givet os personlige oplysninger, bedes du kontakte os, så vi kan håndtere det.

Ændringer af denne politik

Vi kan opdatere denne privatlivspolitik fra tid til anden. Eventuelle ændringer vil blive offentliggjort på denne side med en opdateret revisionsdato.
